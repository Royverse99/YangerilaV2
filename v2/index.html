Timeline

of the Academy

Past

Yangerila Creative Studio was born from a teacher's vision to make guitar learning more effective, progressive, and enjoyable for everyone. With this vision, our head guitar coach Micky Dixit, after teaching at multiple institutes, started his own guitar classes, 'Rockford Academy Of Music,' in 2011. In 2017, the academy was rebranded as a Guitar Specialty academy, giving birth to the new identity: 'Yangerila Creative Studio.' With over a decade of teaching experience, we have developed innovative teaching styles and our own courses to ensure students receive the best learning experience and care.

Present

This year, in 2025, Yangerila brings over 20 years of experience not just in teaching guitar, but in exploring and experimenting to make guitar learning an amazing journey for everyone. We have developed our 'Advanced Modular Grading Structure,' a structured course system that accelerates learning and helps students make guitar a part of their life. Whether a school student, working professional, or homemaker, our courses are designed so everyone can learn, enjoy, and fall in love with this instrument.

Future

Looking ahead, we will soon launch our learning app, which will not only host classes but also serve as a hub for students and musicians to interact, creating a thriving community of excellence. We are adding more graded courses for different instruments with the finest teachers. In the future, we dream of Yangerila being a one-stop hub for all music learning, offering courses for every aspect of music. Our vision is for students to experience the most advanced learning through our innovative and creative teaching methods.
