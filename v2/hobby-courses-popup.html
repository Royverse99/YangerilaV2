Hobby Courses

Learn guitar for fun, flexibility, and confidence

One of our most popular programs, Hobby Courses are designed for working professionals, homemakers, and music lovers who want to enjoy the guitar without the pressure of deep theory or advanced techniques. These courses focus on practical playing skills, helping you learn your favorite songs faster and enjoy performing with friends and family.

Hobby Courses are built around two simple principles:

1. You should be able to play your favorite songs independently, without needing constant help from a teacher or online tutorials.
2. You should feel confident enough to perform in front of friends, family, or even at small gatherings.

About the Course:

Hobby Course 1 is designed for students who want to enjoy playing guitar without diving too deep into music theory. The goal is to get you performing your favorite songs as quickly as possible. Whether you’re a homemaker, working professional, or someone who just wants a creative outlet, this course makes sure learning guitar feels fun, flexible, and rewarding.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Core rhythm-building skills to play songs with steady timing
  • Open chord shapes (major, minor, and a few 7th chords)
  • Smooth left- and right-hand coordination
  • Easy strumming patterns in 2/4, 3/4, and 4/4 time signatures
  • Singing while strumming (basic singing tips included)
  • Major and minor scales for understanding melodies
  • How to recognize the scale of a song you’re playing
  • Transposing simple songs into different keys for comfortable singing
  • Playing full songs with chords and rhythm

Upon Completion:

After completing Hobby Course 1, you’ll be able to pick up the guitar and confidently play songs of your choice. You’ll know how to strum in rhythm, switch chords smoothly, and even sing along while playing. Most importantly, you’ll be independent—able to play without depending on a teacher, tabs, or online tutorials. Perfect for small performances with friends, family, or colleagues, this course turns learning into a joyful hobby.
