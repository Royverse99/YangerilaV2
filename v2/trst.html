How does the Advanced Modular Grading Structure work? ▶
To understand the Advanced Modular Grading Structure, we first need to understand the guitar. Guitar isn’t just one instrument—it includes electric, acoustic, classical, and more. On top of that, there are many techniques you can play: Rhythm, Lead, Fingerpicking, Percussive styles, and others. At Yangerila, we believe a student should be fluent in playing multiple techniques to become a well-rounded guitarist. Our Modular Grading Structure achieves this by dividing each technique into small, manageable grades. Students can then choose their next course based on their musical interests and guidance from their counsellor or teacher, allowing them to become their own unique musician. Other grading structures, if taught at other academies, follow a fixed path designed for an average student, often teaching only one technique and restricting flexibility. Our approach ensures freedom, variety, and command on the instrument.
What are Yangerila’s Smart Sheets?▶
No education is complete without proper course material. And if you already have an amazing course, then adding resources that support learning makes it truly exceptional. That’s the vision behind Yangerila’s Smart Sheets. Smart Sheets are a full, comprehensive set of course materials, organized into individual lesson sheets. As the course progresses, students receive the relevant sheets in their Batch Hub, accessible anytime, anywhere—no notebooks or printouts needed. Each sheet is enriched with supporting resources like supporting audios, explanatory videos, and helpful tips, making practice more efficient and enhancing the overall learning experience. This took us years to build, but it makes learning highly organized and effortless for students.
